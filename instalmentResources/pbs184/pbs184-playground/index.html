PBS 183 SASS Playground

This is a sample HTML page for experimenting with SASS.

To use this page, add your SASS code to `index.scss`, then compile it to `index.css` with the command:

sass index.scss index.css

Wisdom

Wisdom is previous and often comes from people who think deeply about the universe as it actually exists.

Scientists of all types, and most especially those that broke the mold are often very insightful, wise and worth listening to.

"The world is a dangerous place to live; not because of the people who are evil, but because of the people who don't do anything about it."

— Albert Einstein (pacifist and humanist)

"Nothing in life is to be feared, it is only to be understood. Now is the time to understand more, so that we may fear less."

— Marie Curie (pioneering chemist and first female Nobel Laureate)

"Sometimes it is the people no one imagines anything of who do the things that no one can imagine."

— Alan Turing (pioneering computer scientist and mathematician who happend to be gay)
